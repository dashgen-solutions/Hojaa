## How to Create GIFs

Use any screen recorder — recommended:
- **macOS**: Built-in Screen Recording (Cmd+Shift+5) then convert to GIF
- **Cross-platform**: [Kap](https://getkap.co/) or [LICEcap](https://www.cockos.com/licecap/)
- **Online**: [ezgif.com](https://ezgif.com/video-to-gif) to convert video to GIF

Place assets in this `assets/` directory and reference them in README.md.
